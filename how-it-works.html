Platform Guidelines

Follow these simple steps to keep earning on Windsor Grove

Please read all guidelines carefully to ensure a smooth and rewarding experience on our platform.

1
Account Reset
  • To reset your account, you must first complete all improvement tasks using the bonus received after registration.
  • The minimum amount required for reset is $50 USD (excluding your account balance).
  • You must contact customer service to request an account reset after completing the first set of tasks.
2
Withdrawals & Security of Funds
  • Users must complete all optimization tasks before meeting system withdrawal requirements.
  • Withdrawals are processed automatically by the system to prevent loss of funds.
  • Users' funds are secure, and the platform will be liable for any accidental loss.
3
Account Security
  • Do not share your account password or security code with anyone. The platform is not responsible for losses due to disclosure.
  • Set strong passwords that do not include personal details like birthdays or phone numbers.
  • If you forget your password, you can reset it by contacting online customer service.
4
Optimization Tasks
  • Tasks are assigned randomly by the system and cannot be changed, canceled, or skipped.
  • Due to the large number of users, product assignments are handled by the system automatically.
5
Legal Compliance
  • Legal action will be taken in cases of account misuse.
6
Deposits
  • Each item comes from a different merchant, and deposits should not be held for more than 30 minutes.
  • Before making a deposit, confirm the merchant's USD address with customer service.
7
Deposit Responsibility
  • The platform is not responsible for deposits made to the wrong account.
8
Task Completion Time
  • Users must complete improvement tasks within 8 hours.
  • If a task is not completed and no extension is requested, the user may receive complaints from merchants, resulting in a lower credit score.
